Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of laptops do you sell?

We offer a wide range of laptops, including business laptops, gaming laptops, ultrabooks, 2-in-1 laptops, student laptops, and refurbished laptops.

2. Do you provide warranties?

Yes, all new laptops come with a manufacturer’s warranty, typically ranging from 1 to 3 years. Refurbished laptops come with a shop warranty of 6 months.

3. Can I trade in my old laptop?

Yes, we accept trade-ins for selected laptops. The value depends on the model, condition, and market demand.

4. Do you offer financing or EMI options?

Yes, we offer flexible financing and EMI options through our financial partners. Terms and conditions apply.

5. Do you provide laptop repair and maintenance services?

Yes, we provide services like screen replacement, battery replacement, keyboard repair, software troubleshooting, and data recovery.

6. Can I customize my laptop?

Some models support upgrades like additional RAM or SSD. We can help you with customization options for your specific needs.

7. Do you offer discounts for students or bulk purchases?

Yes, we have special discounts for students with valid ID and for bulk purchases. Contact us for more details.

8. What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 15 days of purchase for laptops in their original condition. Returns may be subject to a restocking fee.

9. Do you sell laptop accessories?

Yes, we offer accessories like laptop bags, chargers, external storage, mouse and keyboards, and headphones.

10. How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us via phone, email, or visit our store. Check our website for more details.
